# All-in-One Catering Business Platform

## Customer Meeting Notes (Raw)
A catering/food-service operator currently juggles multiple disconnected systems and manual processes:

### Current Stack & Pain Points
- **Bookbee ($50/mo):** Handles quotes, invoices, and payments — but no loyalty system, no referral tracking, no payroll
- **Client tracking:** Done in a physical notebook. No CRM.
- **Employee time tracking:** Needs clock-in/clock-out tied to payroll. Currently not integrated.
- **Video monitoring:** Watches video to check on operations/staff
- **Customer inquiries come from everywhere:** Social media DMs, texts, the Bookbee link — no single intake funnel

### What They Want
1. **Track client invoices** — consolidated, not scattered
2. **Loyalty points system** — reward repeat customers
3. **Referral program** — track and incentivize referrals
4. **Payroll** — linked to employee clock-in/clock-out
5. **Single platform** — stop paying for and juggling multiple tools
6. **Inquiry & booking system** — customer-facing link showing menu/prices, ability to add one-off orders, send an inquiry with date, time, location, setup details (captures all the info people forget)
7. **Unified customer intake** — can't go off social media alone because some customers just text

### Key Insight
This operator is spending $50/mo on Bookbee alone, tracking clients in a notebook, and losing information across channels. They want ONE system that handles: CRM → quoting → booking → invoicing → payments → loyalty → referrals → payroll → employee management.

## Target Customer Profile
Small-to-medium catering operators, mobile food businesses, event food service providers who have outgrown notebook + Bookbee but can't afford or justify enterprise catering software.
